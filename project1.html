GPU Benchmark Analysis

This project explores various GPU benchmarks to understand performance dynamics and assist in making informed purchasing decisions. We analyze GPUs based on 3D and 2D benchmark scores, performance per dollar, and thermal design power across different categories.

Dataset Overview

This dataset features benchmark scores and specifications for GPUs, including 3D and 2D performance scores, price, performance value, and thermal design power (TDP), sourced from a comprehensive GPU benchmark database.

Data Pre-processing

Data pre-processing included cleaning duplicate entries, handling missing values by replacing them with median values, and ensuring correct data types for analysis, particularly converting dates into datetime format.

Visualizations and Insights

Visual analysis revealed that higher TDP is generally associated with higher performance scores, and that GPU pricing does not always correlate linearly with performance, highlighting the importance of selecting GPUs based on both price and specifications.

Impact of Analysis

While this analysis aids consumers and manufacturers alike by highlighting performance benchmarks and cost efficiency, it also raises concerns about its impact on market dynamics and pricing strategies. Additionally, the lack of data on long-term reliability could mislead decisions based solely on performance metrics.
